Kai

Kai, once Master Oogway’s closest friend, fell into darkness and became obsessed with stealing chi. After centuries trapped in the Spirit Realm, he has returned, wielding jade warriors and seeking ultimate power by draining the energy of every kung fu master.

Lord Shen

Lord Shen, the cunning and ruthless peacock, sought to conquer China with his powerful firework-based weapons. Blinded by ambition, he tried to change his destiny by wiping out pandas, only to be stopped by Po, the very panda he feared.

Tai Lung

Tai Lung, the powerful snow leopard, was once Shifu’s most gifted student. Consumed by pride and denied the Dragon Scroll, he turned into a fearsome villain. With unmatched strength and skill, he challenged the Valley of Peace, only to meet his fate at the paws of Po.
